Lead Event Coordinator·UC Irvine·2024

Four events. Four different concepts. One team. The Supper Club @ UCI was an exercise in turning a good idea into a real experience, from planning the menu and coordinating grocery runs to organizing prep and making sure service ran clean.

4Events
230+Students Fed
4Concepts
01

Plaza Verde BBQ

100 Guests·Outdoor BBQ

The first event and the biggest. A full outdoor BBQ at Plaza Verde that drew 100 students and immediately set the bar for everything that followed. Planning the menu, coordinating grocery runs, and managing prep and grill logistics for a crowd of 100 on a debut event was the steepest learning curve, and the one that shaped how the rest of the series was run.

Menu
  • Wagyu Smash Burgers
  • Elote Corn
  • Garden Salad
  • French Onion Bread

02

Autumn Dinner

40 Guests·Multi-Course

The most ambitious menu of the four. A multi-course sit-down dinner built around seasonal ingredients, from dry aged duck to sweet potato crème brûlée. Menu planning, grocery coordination, and prep team organization all came together for one night.

Menu
  • Fennel salad
  • Three dips with baguettes
  • Butternut squash pasta
  • Dry aged duck
  • Sweet potato crème brûlée

03

Curry Night

50 Guests·Japanese Curry

A more intimate event built around one dish done right. Curry Night brought 50 students together for a focused dinner that leaned into warmth and comfort. A smaller guest list meant tighter execution: precise grocery coordination, a streamlined prep team, and a menu that didn't need variety to feel complete.

Menu
  • Beef Japanese Curry
  • Vegan Japanese Curry

04

Dawn Way Cafe

40 Guests·Café Style

The final event took a café format: a relaxed, drop-in experience for 40 students to eat, hang out, and stay as long as they wanted. After three events, the logistics were smoother and the team was in sync. The café felt like the series finding its stride.

Menu
  • Brown Butter Banana Bread
  • Glazed Matcha Pound Cake
  • Strawberry Basque Cheesecake
  • Matcha Tiramisu
  • Matcha Einspänner
